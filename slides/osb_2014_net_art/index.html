Net Art:

What's Cool About Art on the Net?

Here's What's Cool:

  • Uses browser as medium
  • Distributed over the internet, not through a gallery
  • Intent of the art is often internet-related
  • Access to all of the awesome features of the web as a platform
- Emphasize that the work is often inextricably part of the browser - Mention how revolutionary internet distribution is to the art world - Mention that the art often questions how we use the internet, and what the internet is doing to our attitudes, social lives, senses of self, etc. - Emphasize that the web as a platform is being developed and expanded every day, so the medium rapidly changes and improves. This is not a feature you get with, say, painting.

Vince Mckelvie - Honey

  • WebGL - on cutting edge of browser tech
  • Cool visual display to go with audio track
  • Somewhat interactive
  • Good example of Net Art aesthetic

Banger Briz - Three.js Playground

  • More WebGL
  • Fully interactive, participatory art
  • Users are involved in producing the "art object"
  • Or should I say, the "art experience"
  • Shareable, possible to return to previous creations

Rafael Rozendaal - if no yes . com
