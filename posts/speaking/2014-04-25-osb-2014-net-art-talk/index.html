Net Art Praxis - Making Internet-Based Visual Art Using Open Source (link to slides)

I gave this talk at Open Source Bridge 2014. Thanks again to everyone for coming! Here is a list of extra examples, for those who were curious to check out some other interesting projects.

Things that were discussed during the question-and-answer session:

  • Carmin Karasic's Portfolio of Early Net Artworks
  • Rihanna SNL Performance Controversy
  • OpenProcessing.org - a place to share and view cool Processing Sketches
  • Oregon City Municipal Elevator Projection Art Project
  • Unnumbered Sparks, large interactive installation in Vancouver, CA

More neat net art projects, in no particular order:

  • River Of The Net
  • klear.me
  • The Silva Field Guide to Birds of a Parallel Future
  • Live Code Lab
  • Club Rothko Builder
  • Patatap.com
  • Zen Photon Garden
  • George and Jonathan's Songs, Visualized
  • Interactive Haiku - Call for Projects
  • Infinite Sunset
  • Webcam Mesh
  • For Your Processing - Cool Collection of Gifs and other projects made with the Processing framework
